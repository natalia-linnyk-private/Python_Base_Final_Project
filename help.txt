                     List of available commands:
----------------- Commands to work with Address Book ----------------------
add            - adds contact to address book,
                 example of input: add [contact name] [phone]
change         - changes existing contact's phone,
                 example of input: change [contact name] [phone]
remove         - removes contact from the book,
                 example of input: remove [contact name]
find-by-email  - finds and show all information about the contact by email,
                 example of input: find-by-email [email]
find-by-name   - finds and show all information about the contact by name,
                 example of input: find-by-name [contact name]
phone          - returns phone number by name,
                 example of input: phone [contact name]
remove-phone   - removes a phone from the record,
                  example of input: remove_phone [contact name] [phone]
add-email      - adds email to existing contact,
                 example of input: add-email [contact name] [email]
email          - returns email by name,
                 example of input: email [contact name]
remove-email   - removes an email from the record,
                 example of input: remove_email [contact name] [email]
add-address    - adds address to existing contact or update it if address already exists,
                 example of input: add-address [contact name] [address data]
remove-address - removes address from contact,
                 example of input: remove-address [contact name]
all            - shows all added contacts,
                 example of input: all
add-birthday   - adds birthday date to the selected contact in format DD.MM.YYYY,
                 example of input: add-birthday [contact name] [birthday]
show-birthday  - shows birthday date of the selected contact,
                 example of input: show-birthday [contact name]
birthdays      - shows all contacts that have birthdays during selected count of
                 days (during next week by default), example of input: birthdays

-------------------------- Commands to work with Notes -----------------------------
add-note   - adds a note, example of input: add [note title]
edit-note  - edits an existing note, example of input: edit-note [note ID]
all-notes  - shows all note titles by ID, example of input: all-notes
add-tag    - adds a tag to a note, example of input: add-tag [note ID] [tag]
remove-tag - removes a tag from a note, example of input: remove-tag [note ID] [tag]
find-notes - finds notes by tag and sorts them by one of params (id, title, created_at, updated_at),
             example of input: find-notes [tag] [sort param]
remove-note - deletes a note, example of input: delete-note [note ID]

----------- General commands -----------------------
hello - says hello, example of input: hello
close / exit - finish dialog with assistant bot
help - show main menu again (list of available commands)
